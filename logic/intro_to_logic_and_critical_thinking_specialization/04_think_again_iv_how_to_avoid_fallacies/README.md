# Think Again IV: How to Avoid Fallacies

## Course

This course covers common fallacies, including fallacies of unclarity, relevance, vacuity, circularity, and strategies for refuting unsuccessful arguments.

## Suggested Reading

Understanding Arguments, Ninth Edition, Concise, Chapters 13-17.

## Modules

- Welcome to the Course
- Fallacies of Unclarity
- Fallacies of Relevance
- Fallacies of Vacuity and Circularity
- Refutation: Its Varieties and Pitfalls
- Catch-Up and Final Quiz
